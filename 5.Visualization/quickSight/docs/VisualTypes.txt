Visual Types:
    -AutoGraph:
        - Just Select the columns and quicksight will decide what kind of graph is the best to represent that information
            based of the type of the data

    -Bar Charts:
        - Did for comparisons and data distribution (Histogram)
        
    -Line graphs:
        - Changes over time

    -Scatter plots, heat maps:
        - Correlation between two or more variables

    -Pie graphs, tree maps:
        - Aggregations

    -Pivot tables:
        - Intersections between attributes
        - Indexing tabular data

    -Stories: 
        - Create a narrative specifying how is the behavior of the data through the story
        - Evolution of an analysis for collaboration

    -KPIs
        - Performance Indicators

    -Geospatial Charts (maps)
