What is QuickSight?
    -Serverless solution to build visualizations, perform ad-hoc analysis
        Check visualizations anytime, on any device
    -Can extract exposed data



Data Sources:
    -Redshift
    -Aurora / RDS
    -Athena
    -EC2-hosted databases
    -Files (s3 or on premises)
        -Excel
        -CSV, TSV
        -Common or extended log format
    -Data preparation allows limited ETL
    -Is important to perform ETL before visualizations, for example change field names of a csv
        to perform better visualizations
